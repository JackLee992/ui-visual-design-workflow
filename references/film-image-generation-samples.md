Use this reference when the user wants a live-action film still, TV drama promotional photo, short-drama cover, or FMV-style visual rather than game CG, anime, digital painting, or concept art.

## Test Case: Han Fantasy Romance Film Still

Goal: test whether the skill can move from a game-CG direction into a realistic cinematic costume-drama direction while preserving a sensual but non-explicit character brief.

Reference direction: an original clearly adult Han-dynasty-inspired Chinese woman in a fantasy romance cultivation setting, photographed like a live-action costume drama promo still. Do not copy a specific actress, character, franchise poster, costume, title treatment, logo, or visual identity.

Safety and originality constraints:

- Character must be clearly adult.
- Sensual styling is allowed through fitted tailoring, tube-top styling, short layered skirt, shoulder-draped gauze, pose, and cinematic lighting.
- Keep opaque coverage; no exposed nipples, exposed genitals, transparent nudity, explicit sexual acts, or pornographic posing.
- Avoid exact likenesses of existing actresses and exact costumes from existing dramas, games, or films.
