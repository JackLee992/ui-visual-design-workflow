# Game Image Generation Samples

Use this reference only when the user asks for examples, sample prompts, or tested game image generation patterns.

## Test Case: Sci-Fi Action Heroine

Goal: test whether the skill can move from lightweight art direction to explicit image generation mode for game marketing assets.

Reference direction: a glamorous adult sci-fi female action heroine, inspired by the broad appeal of Korean console action game heroines, without copying a specific character face, costume, logo, franchise symbol, or visual identity.

Safety and originality constraints:

- Character must be clearly adult.
- Sensual styling is allowed through deep V tailoring, transparent smart-fabric panels, pose, lighting, and silhouette.
- Keep strategic opaque coverage; no exposed nipples, exposed genitals, explicit sexual acts, or pornographic posing.
- Do not copy Eve from Stellar Blade, any exact costume, official logo, UI, or franchise symbol.

## Sample 1: Key Art

Use for: splash art, poster art, store hero art, campaign announcement art.

Prompt:

```text
Create a premium AAA sci-fi action game key art image with stronger sensual glamour while staying non-explicit. Subject: an original adult sci-fi female warrior, glamorous and athletic, confident seductive expression, powerful heroine energy, clearly adult. Outfit: sleek black and silver futuristic combat bodysuit with a dramatic deep V neckline, sheer translucent armored mesh panels over the torso and hips, opaque strategic coverage, no nipples visible, no genitals visible, no nudity; high-fashion cyber armor fused with battle-ready tactical gear. Intent: premium action game key art inspired by Korean console sci-fi action aesthetics, without copying any existing character. Composition: full-body hero pose, low camera angle, elegant contrapposto stance emphasizing waistline and athletic curves, character dominates center-right, ruined neon future city behind her, huge ambiguous enemy silhouette in far background, strong clean silhouette. Camera/lens/framing: cinematic 35mm, vertical hero framing, polished poster composition, dramatic depth. Lighting: cold cyan rim light tracing the transparent fabric edges, blue energy glow from weapon and armor seams, warm battlefield sparks. Palette/value: glossy graphite black, silver white, cyan blue, small crimson danger accents. Style/medium: realistic high-detail AAA game key art, polished concept art, premium game marketing visual. Materials/details: flexible combat armor, carbon fiber panels, translucent smart-fabric mesh, glossy latex-like armored surfaces, subtle battle wear, glowing energy blade, precise mechanical details. Mood: sexy, dangerous, elegant, fast, high-stakes combat. Platform crop: works in 16:9 hero and 4:5 poster crop. Text policy: no text in image. Negative constraints: no nudity, no exposed nipples, no exposed genitals, no explicit sexual act, no pornographic pose, no copied Eve face, no exact Stellar Blade costume, no franchise logo, no watermark, no readable text.
```

QA notes:

- Works best when the main need is character fantasy and premium campaign energy.
- Keep one strong body silhouette and one weapon/energy focal line.
- If the result is too conservative, strengthen fashion terms such as "dramatic deep V neckline" and "translucent smart-fabric mesh"; if it becomes too explicit, add stronger opaque coverage constraints.

## Sample 2: Store Capsule

Use for: Steam-style capsule, store banner, horizontal promotional card, feature tile.

Prompt:

```text
Create a horizontal game store capsule / promotional banner with stronger sensual glamour while staying non-explicit. Subject: an original adult futuristic female action hero, elegant, sexy, glamorous, athletic, battle-ready, not an existing character. Outfit: sleek futuristic black and silver combat bodysuit with dramatic deep V neckline, sheer translucent smart-fabric panels, glossy armored cups and opaque strategic coverage, no nipples visible, no nudity; high-fashion cyber armor with tactical combat credibility. Intent: game store capsule art with very strong thumbnail readability and high allure. Composition: half-body portrait on the left, confident three-quarter pose with clear waist and shoulder line, clean dark negative space on the right for a future game title, simple bold silhouette, no actual title text. Camera/lens/framing: medium close-up, slight low angle, face and upper torso readable at tiny size, energy blade hilt or glowing weapon detail visible but not cluttered. Lighting: hard cyan rim light outlining hair, neckline, and transparent fabric edges; soft controlled face light; high contrast against dark graphite background. Palette/value: dark graphite background, glossy black armor, silver highlights, cyan glow, subtle crimson warning accent. Style/medium: polished AAA sci-fi action promotional art, realistic high-detail render, premium console action game marketing. Materials/details: streamlined tactical bodysuit, metallic shoulder armor, carbon fiber texture, translucent mesh panels, glowing armor seams, clean mechanical accents. Mood: premium, dangerous, alluring, precise, cyber action heroine tone. Platform crop: wide horizontal capsule, title-safe clean area on right, readable at small thumbnail size. Text policy: no text in image. Negative constraints: no official logo, no watermark, no readable text, no exposed nipples, no exposed genitals, no explicit sexual act, no pornographic pose, no copied Eve face, no exact Stellar Blade costume, no tiny busy details behind title area.
```

QA notes:

- The capsule prompt must preserve a clean title-safe area.
- At small size, a simple head/torso silhouette usually beats full-body detail.
- Avoid adding text inside the generated image; add typography later in a design tool.
